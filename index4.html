Aarogya Care: Diagnostic Testing at Home Across India

Aarogya Care is India’s leading at-home health test service, offering an extensive range of diagnostic tests across 250+ cities—and rapidly expanding. Backed by a robust network of fully automated, state-of-the-art laboratories and a highly trained team of phlebotomists, Aarogya Care brings reliable health check-ups right to your doorstep.

Innovation lies at the core of Aarogya Care. We have transformed the traditional diagnostic experience by embedding smart technology into every step of the process—making it faster, more seamless, and highly accurate.

From booking your test to receiving your reports, our tech-enabled systems ensure a smooth and hassle-free journey. Our proprietary innovations such as SmartPrik™ (tamper-proof sample collection kits), pre-barcoded vials, and CoolSure™ (live temperature-monitored transport boxes) guarantee that your samples are collected and processed under optimal conditions.

To ensure precision and reliability, Aarogya Care follows a tech-driven quality control framework powered by advanced monitoring tools, AI-based systems, and remote supervision. With rigorous training and modern devices, we've standardized diagnostics—so you receive trustworthy, accurate reports every time.

At Aarogya Care, we’re not just offering health tests—we’re shaping the future of home diagnostics in India.
